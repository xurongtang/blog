complex sentences

sentence 1

In Florida, in the case involving Trump’s alleged hoarding of classified documents, Judge Aileen Cannon and Jack Smith, the special counsel, have been engaged in a bitter fight over jury instructions—even though there is as yet no jury, let alone a trial date
在佛罗里达州，在涉及特朗普涉嫌囤积机密文件的案件中，艾琳·坎农法官和特别检察官杰克·史密斯就陪审团指示展开了激烈的斗争——尽管目前还没有陪审团，更不用说审判日期了

sentence 2

Once sworn in, that jury—the first to be impanelled in a criminal trial of a former President—might at last give some fixed form to the jumbled legal picture.
一旦宣誓就职，陪审团——第一个在前总统刑事审判中被排除的陪审团——可能最终会为混乱的法律画面提供某种固定的形式

sentence 3

It is a challenge to the Department of Justice’s use of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act in hundreds of January 6th cases
这是对司法部在 1 月 6 日数百起案件中使用《萨班斯-奥克斯利法案》的挑战.

sentence 4

he is immune to criminal charges arising from acts within the “outer perimeter” of his official duties
他不会因在其公务“外围”范围内的行为而受到刑事指控。

sentence 5

the Justices will be asking whether the D.C. trial should be gutted, or should even take place at all.
大法官们将询问是否应该取消华盛顿特区的审判，或者甚至根本不应该进行审判。

sentence 6

despite an almost constant frenzy of legal action, it can seem as though very little has actually happened.
尽管法律诉讼几乎持续不断，但实际发生的事情似乎很少。

sentence 7

Judge Cannon, a Trump appointee who is new to the bench, has not been quick to rule on motions, and has issued orders that are confusing or have alarmed prosecutors because they suggest that she might be ready to accept some of Trump’s more farcical legal arguments.
坎农法官是特朗普任命的新法官，她并没有迅速对动议作出裁决，并发布了令人困惑或令检察官震惊的命令，因为他们暗示她可能已经准备好接受特朗普的一些更滑稽的法律论点。

sentence 8

There is a kind of justice in Trump’s being told to sit still and behave (and being sanctioned if he doesn’t), but comeuppance can’t be the main point.
特朗普被告知要安静地坐着、按规矩行事（如果他不这样做就会受到制裁），这是一种正义，但报应并不是重点。

sentence 9

A case about hush money can’t really offer accountability for an attempted coup; nor can it fully address the understandable, widespread frustration that Trump has secured the Republican nomination despite the weight of the pending cases against him.
关于封口费的案件并不能真正追究未遂政变的责任。 它也不能完全解决人们对特朗普获得共和党提名的普遍不满，这是可以理解的，尽管针对他的悬而未决的案件很重。

sentence 10

The judge found that he had no basis to remove her, but was so sharply critical of her choices.
法官发现他没有依据将她驱逐出境，但却如此尖锐地批评她的选择.
