complex sentences

sentence 1

The group ogled the glassy expanse of water and the snowcapped mountains beyond, and then got to inflating the kayaks, which slowly wheezed to life. “You have to be gentle,” Donnelly said.
这群人凝视着一望无际的水面和远处白雪皑皑的山脉，然后开始给皮划艇充气，皮划艇慢慢地喘息着。 “你必须温柔，”唐纳利说。

sentence 2

As they drifted, Donnelly noticed that Lim’s kayak seemed to be folding in on itself: a leak. “Dex, did you inflate the bottom of that?” he called out.
当他们漂流时，唐纳利注意到林的皮划艇似乎正在自行折叠：漏水了。 “德克斯，你给底部充气了吗？” 他喊道。

sentence 3

He has lived at the edge of Death Valley for about a decade. He sees himself as one of the area’s few chroniclers of climate change and of rare desert phenomena.
他在死亡谷边缘生活了大约十年。 他认为自己是该地区为数不多的气候变化和罕见沙漠现象的记录者之一。
