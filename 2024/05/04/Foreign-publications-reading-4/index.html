complex sentences

sentence 1

Frustrated in his own work, he decided to demystify the artistic process by interviewing more than forty artists working in different disciplines, including Stephen Sondheim, Kara Walker, and Sofia Coppola.
由于对自己的工作感到沮丧，他决定通过采访四十多名不同学科的艺术家来揭开艺术过程的神秘面纱，其中包括斯蒂芬·桑德海姆、卡拉·沃克和索菲亚·科波拉。

sentence 2

I had been talking to all these other artists who were basically making things that would last forever.
我一直在和所有其他艺术家交谈，他们基本上正在制作可以永远存在的东西。

sentence 3

It was the making that consumed them, and many were kind of indifferent to the results. So I thought, Well, sandcastles are a pure example, because they disappear
制作过程消耗了他们的精力，许多人对结果漠不关心。 所以我想，好吧，沙堡就是一个纯粹的例子，因为它们消失了。

sentence 4

Adelman smoothed the side of the tower with his palm, then cut out a winding crevasse, blowing away excess grains with a metal straw
阿德尔曼用手掌抚平了塔的侧面，然后凿出了一条蜿蜒的裂缝，用金属吸管吹走了多余的谷物。

sentence 5

I expected it to last a little bit longer than that.
我预计它会持续比这更长的时间。
